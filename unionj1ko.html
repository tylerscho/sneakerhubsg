Union Air Jordan 1 Low KO

Description

The Union Air Jordan 1 Low KO is a collaboration between Jordan Brand and Los Angeles-based streetwear brand Union. This unique iteration of the classic Air Jordan 1 features a distressed leather upper, with a mix of blue and white hues. The shoe also features Union's signature "Off-Noir" tag on the tongue, and "Union LA" branding on the insole.

Release Information

The Union Air Jordan 1 Low KO was released on February 8th, 2023 and is available in limited quantities.
