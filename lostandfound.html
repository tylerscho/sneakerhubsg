Air Jordan 1 High 'Lost and Found'

The original Air Jordan 1 Chicago colorway was first introduced in 1985 and has only been retroed a few times since. But 2022 is the year that the colorway returns with an added vintage look. Pre-yellowed accents and cracked leather uppers showcase a fabricated look of age and wear.
